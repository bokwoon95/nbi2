Welcome to the Dashboard

This is a responsive layout with a collapsible sidebar. When you resize your browser window to be narrower than 768px, the sidebar will fold into a hamburger menu icon.

Features

The layout includes:

  • Fixed header with hamburger menu
  • Collapsible sidebar navigation
  • Responsive main content area
  • Smooth transitions and hover effects

How to Use

On mobile devices or narrow screens, click the hamburger icon in the top-left corner to toggle the sidebar. Click on any menu item to navigate through different sections.

Content Area

This main content area scrolls independently and adapts to the available space. The sidebar remains accessible but doesn't take up precious screen real estate on smaller devices.
